Cycle the Continental Divide Annual Event

Every year we host the incredible Cycle the Continental Divide event. This is a wonder event for dedicated cyclists. You take a long journay across of the the continent divides of the United States. It is a long journey so we will follow along in a car to keep you safe and carry all of your food and equipment so you don't have to worry about anything.

  • Held from the 1st day of June to the last day of July every year
  • You can one of four routes.
    • Great
    • Great Basin
    • Eastern
    • St. Lawrence
  • $1,000 fee
