Grand Tumble

Grand Tumble is on of the most intense tours we have. It is very rocky and bumpy. This tour does not have much vegetation, which is good because you will need to see the trail. The slope of the trail is varied from steep to flat.

  • 30 Miles
  • Expert Difficulty
  • Located in the Crazy Mountains
  • $200 fee

Smooth Sailing

Smooth Sailing is one of our beginning tours. You don't have to worry about rough terrain jostling you around. The slope is not very steep either. This tour is great for new or casual bikers. There is a great deal of vegetation for you to watch

  • 15 Miles
  • Easy Difficulty
  • Located in the Crazy Mountains
  • $50 fee
